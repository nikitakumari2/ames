## Overview

**ÂMES** is a first-of-its-kind wellness fragrance concept for L'Oréal Luxe. It transforms daily scent into a personalised nervous system ritual through four emotion-matched elemental fragrances, a smart refillable glass vessel, and an AI companion app that adapts to your physiology in real time.

ÂMES proposes an entirely new luxury category — **Wellness Perfumery** — deployable across L'Oréal Luxe brands including Prada, YSL, Armani, and Valentino.

---

## The Product

### The Smart Vessel
- Recycled ultra-clear glass, built to last 5+ years
- 4 snap-in refill cartridges (one per element)
- NFC chip for zero-battery refill tracking and anti-counterfeiting
- Thermochromic touch-response coating

### The AI Companion App
- Reads HRV, sleep data, and voice biomarkers
- Prescribes your daily elemental blend based on nervous system state
- Adapts over time to neurodivergent profiles and diverse physiology
- Links retail, DTC, and e-commerce into one loyalty ecosystem

### The Four Elements

| Element | Mood | Scent Profile |
|---------|------|---------------|
| 🜃 Earth | Grounding | Deep vetiver and dark amber |
| 🜄 Water | Clarity | Cool sea minerals and white tea |
| 🜁 Air | Lightness | Airy neroli and yuzu |
| 🜂 Fire | Vitality | Warm saffron and oud |

## Why It Wins

**Sustainable**
- 70% recycled ultra-clear glass vessel
- 60% less packaging vs. conventional fragrance
- 45% carbon footprint reduction over a 3-year lifecycle
- Directly aligned with L'Oréal's −20% packaging intensity goal by 2030

**Inclusive**
- 100% ungendered — emotional state, not identity, drives the product
- Biometric AI adapts to neurodivergent profiles and diverse physiology
- Accessible entry at €220 launch; ongoing savings via €85 refill subscription
- Retail staff positioned as wellness guides, not salespeople

**Scalable**
- Architecture deployable across Prada, YSL, Armani, and Valentino
- Wearable integrations (Apple Watch, Oura, Whoop) scale to any market
- Neuro-Scent Bar retail concept replicable globally — modelled on Aesop Sensorium
- Digital-first app enables borderless reach from Day 1
